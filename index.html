    Use the planner effectively

    Break large tasks down

    Instead of writing “study for exam,” create smaller tasks such as “review lecture 3” or “solve practice problems.”

    Prioritize urgent work

    Use the High priority label for tasks that are important or close to the deadline.

    Review your progress

    At the end of the day, check completed tasks and decide what should be done next.
